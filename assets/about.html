About

In 2005 or 2006 I recieved a copy of a DreamSpell calendar from our local food co-op in Olympia. This began quite an interesting journey as, being a computer programmer with a degree in English literature, I had finally found a "system" that could be used to help understand or explain with a model our life experience.

I have always been interested in nature, numbers and colors. I remember my favorite discussion durng a highschool English class, a substitute teacher brought a college-level lecture on the usage of color in The Great Gatsby, by F. Scott Fitzgerald. As my brother says, I have a "poetical" relationship with numbers. And while growing up, I wanted to be a Naturalist after being immersed and inspired by the wonderful children's books my parents provided.

This is a DreamSpell application for the Android, inspired and adapted from many resources, primarily and materialy from Star Root's amazing DreamSpell Oracle. Credit goes to all who have created and prepared the way (I am quite positive this list is incomplete, but it is a start, look for updates and/or please contact me if you wish to be listed):

Thank you and I hope you enjoy, please send me feedback if you like.
